English Translation (from tanakh.db):
   v1: For the leader; on the gittith. A psalm of David.
   v2: O LORD, our Lord,
How majestic is Your name throughout the earth,
You who have covered the heavens with Your splendor!
   v3: From the mouths of infants and sucklings
You have founded strength on account of Your foes,
to put an end to enemy and avenger.
   v4: When I behold Your heavens, the work of Your fingers,
the moon and stars that You set in place,
   v5: what is man that You have been mindful of him,
mortal man that You have taken note of him,
