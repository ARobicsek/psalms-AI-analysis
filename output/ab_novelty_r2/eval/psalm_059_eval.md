## Failure audit

### Commentary A
**OVERREACH**
- "the distance from being watched to giving praise turns out to be exactly **one letter wide**" — the thesis-takeaway overstates: אֶשְׁמֹרָה (qal) and אֲזַמֵּרָה (piel) differ in binyan and vocalization, not a single letter. A hedges once ("consistent with a near-rhyme") but then repeats the "one letter" framing as the headline conclusion, which is a structural calibration weakness.
- "He builds his final refrain out of the enemy's own verb and then converts it" — the root link is real but "the enemy's own verb" smooths over that v10's אֶשְׁמֹרָה is already the speaker's, not the watchmen's form.
- Al-Mutanabbi "his legend reportedly compelled him to fight to his death" — softened by "reportedly," acceptable but decorative.

**FALSE PROFUNDITY**
- "The dogs come back almost unchanged because dogs circling a city is what relentlessness *sounds* like." — cadence over content.
- "the wrong person is the point" — neat but merely restates the medieval reading.

**LOST HEDGES**
- "the four-stage handover that runs the length of the psalm" — presented as authorial design where it is an interpretive construct (though a defensible one).

### Commentary B
**OVERREACH**
- "a phrase found nowhere else in the Psalter in exactly this form" (אֱלֹקֵי חַסְדִּי) — plausible but a "nowhere else" claim of the kind that invites checking; lightly stated.
- הֵמָּה / יֶהֱמוּ "faint sonic chime ... half-echoes the noise they make" — a stretch presented as observation, the weakest item.

**FALSE PROFUNDITY**
- "The enemies return forever and arrive nowhere; the man they hunted ... reaches it." — elegant, but it does fairly compress the (substantive) aspect argument rather than merely decorate it; borderline.
- "God is the constant; what deepens is the singer's grasp of Him." — epigrammatic, but anchored to the real מִשְׂגַּבִּי observation.

**LOST HEDGES**
- "The curse is enacted textually: what was asked is now described" (v16 fulfilling v12) — stated as fact; reasonable but interpretive.

B's overreaches are individually checkable and minor; A's central takeaway rests on a recurring overstatement.
