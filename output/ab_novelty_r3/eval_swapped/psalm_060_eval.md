**FALSE PROFUNDITY**

**LOST HEDGES**
- "I would commit to 'truth'" on the קֹשֶׁט/קֶשֶׁת crux is honest, but the surrounding framing treats the LXX "bow" as a near-equal that is then dismissed slightly too cleanly.

**LOST HEDGES**

Both are fairly clean. B's strongest claims (Ps 108, צְבָאוֹת, the bow-crux link) are the kind that *could* overreach but are properly anchored.
