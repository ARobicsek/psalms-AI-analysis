(שאל ממני (בני אתה, "Ask of Me"; hence, by virtue of the fact that the terms "inheritance" and "possession" as used here cannot be construed to mean the conquest and subjugation of the nations under the sovereignty of the Jewish throne, the fact that they cannot mean compelling them to obey Him and to His will, and joining oneself with purity are the demands made upon the rulers of the nations through the fact of the existence of the Kingdom that is descended from David, through this kingdom of David ready to conquer, the fact that the ideas and attitudes of nations will fall to the spirit and the doctrine of David and shall remain with it. Thus ירשנו, see Exod. 34:9.

V. 9. (תרועם באר. (כלכי הכליפו חררי ירועם לבע (Zech. 10:1.), means: Make the enlightenment and the moral improvement of the nations the object of your desires and the subject of your prayers. But the fact that this enlightenment and upward improvement of the nations is conceived of in terms of their obedience to God, to the will of God that they must accept, shall be demonstrated by his own deeds and his utterances the implementation of God's will in the moral shaping of the lives of individuals and nations. Thus, by means of his personal example in the implementation of God's will, he shall earnestly understand and take to their hearts the will of God.
